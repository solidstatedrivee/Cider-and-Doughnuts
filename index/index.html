In anticipation of Doughnut Dollies' upcoming West Midtown bakery opening, and just in time for Valentine's and Galentine's festivities. UrbanTree and Dougnut Dollies are teaming up to launch a one-day-only pop up! On Saturday, February 9, Doughnut Dollies will offer a complimentary, mini doughnut "flight" with the purchase of Urban Tree Cidery's custom Dougnut Dollies cider flight: two flights for the price of one! But be sure to arrive early, because the doughnuts are first come first served - and we will run out!! Doughnut Dollies will also be offering a complimentary doughnut with the purchase of UrbanTree's new Hot Buttered R&B cocktail.
